The Creative Brief

Africa specialists carry knowledge that took a decade to earn... And they’ve been giving that knowledge away for free — 6 hours at a time, in spreadsheets and PDFs.

Mufasa’s visual identity must resolve one central tension: feel completely at home in the world Africa specialists sell — warm, rooted, expert, African — while being the most precise, powerful, premium B2B SaaS tool they’ve ever used. These directions each answer that challenge differently. All share the same brand pillars: Speed. Beautiful. Seamless. All are grounded in the Magician + Hero archetype. But each translates the brief into a distinct visual personality.

Senior Brand Recommendation

Direction 01: Savannah Dusk remains the recommended primary direction. It resolves the core tension perfectly — warm enough for Africa specialists, precise enough for a B2B SaaS operating system. The Fraunces + Plus Jakarta Sans type pairing and burnished amber palette create a distinctive, ownable identity.

Next step: Select one direction (or a hybrid) and proceed to full design system build — component library, motion system, and production-ready design tokens.
