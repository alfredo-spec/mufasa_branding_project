# 🦁 Mufasa — Brand Identity Project

**B2B SaaS | Africa Specialist Travel | Brand Identity & Guidelines**

---

## What is this project?

This is the working space for developing the **Mufasa brand identity** — from brand strategy and positioning through to visual identity, design system, and compiled brand guidelines.

Mufasa is a B2B SaaS marketplace that lets Africa specialist travel agents build, price, and present multi-lodge safari itineraries in 30 minutes instead of 6 hours — with live inventory, multi-currency quoting, and digital proposals that close deals.
